About Me

My name is Dukki Hong (홍덕기) and I am originally from South Korea. I am a recent graduate from UC San Diego. I have majored in Statistics and I have a strong interest in data science. To further my passion, I took machine learning and relational database courses on Udemy and I am currently enrolled in UC San Diego's online data science micro-master’s advanced award program at edX.

I have a passion to make a positive impact to the community. I saw myself enjoy working on meaningful projects that help the community and would like to dedicate my career to work on such projects to benefit people around me. I would like to describe myself as outgoing, compassionate and creative. I love collaborating with people from different backgrounds and developing new ideas as I also learn from them.
